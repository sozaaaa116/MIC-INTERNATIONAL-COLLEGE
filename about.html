About This Portal

This exam portal is built for student exam registration, exam result viewing, and student profile management.

What Students Can Do
Register exams, view results, and edit their profile from the student dashboard.
What Staff Can Do
Edit student details, manage registrations, and publish results from the staff dashboard.
Data Storage
This is a demo (frontend-only) project. Data is stored in your browser using LocalStorage, not on a real server.
If you clear browser site data, all accounts and results will be removed.
